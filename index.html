Not only do I seek to spark excitement and interest within CNAS students of all the countless STEM and recreational programs UCR has to offer, I want to become a more capable and responsible role model. College is an incredible opportunity, and now that I am well into my second year at UC Riverside, I truly realize that UCR has allowed me to establish lifelong relationships and connections but has also provided me with various opportunities for research and other scientific and academic endeavors. I want to inspire other CNAS freshman, and as a Peer Mentor, I know can effectively motivate and empower students.
